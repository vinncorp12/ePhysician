About the Founder

Victor Levy

MD | FACG | FACP-Founder

Dr. Levy received his medical degree from State University of New York, and postgraduate training took place at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. Dr. Levy is licensed to practice medicine in New York, Florida and Massachusetts, and is board certified in Internal Medicine and Gastroenterology. He maintained a private practice in consultative gastroenterology for 20 years. He has held multiple hospital administrative positions including President of the Medical Staff and member of the Board of Directors at the largest voluntary hospital system on Florida's Gulf Coast and a recent 'top 50 hospitals' selection, according to US News and World Report and AARP. He held the faculty appointment of Assistant Affiliate Professor of Family Medicine at the University of South Florida School of Medicine.

Pilot studies and related topics have been published in the American Journal of Medicine, American Journal of Gastroenterology, IEEE and presented at the annual scientific meetings of the ACG and AGA.

Speaking engagements have included a presentation to the AHRQ HIT division and the National Emergency Management Summit.
