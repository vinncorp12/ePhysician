Covid Application

How we conquer the problem of an impending influenza pandemic

Addressing the perennial issue of impending influenza pandemics- be they seasonal H1 and H3, or Avian influenza H5- necessitates quickly differentiating between typical clinical presentations, their variants and other respiratory pathogens such as Covid.

This is of particular relevance when there is a delay in, or lack of access to, highly accurate testing- the next step in identification and diagnosis. To better meet both patient-centered and epidemiologic objectives, E-Physician’s Office, Inc., collects, aggregates, assembles and analyzes data at bedside.
