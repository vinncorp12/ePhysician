Introduction of E Physician's Office

Discover your one-stop destination for facility EHR-based, actionable platforms, that are created by physicians, to address a facility's specific clinical pain points- all from the convenience of any desktop or mobile device. Statistical rigor and academic approaches have been utilized with seamless integration into standard clinical processes. Our team works side-by-side with your staff throughout the process, from concept to finished product to feedback and any revision.

ePhysicians Office, Inc. offers a suite of unique services which are unavailable elsewhere within the field of medical informatics. Via a patented e infrastructure, the tenets of rigorous evidence-based decision making - generally regarded to be the domain of academic institutions and biostatisticians - have successfully been democratized and made available to all sectors of the health care community.

The initial step in development of an evidence-based tool is the appropriate assembly of prospectively acquired data. The web side allows the user, from any Internet access, to build a custom template, which automatically accrues data. The data reflects a user-initiated and developed series of variables, variable choices and all potential outcomes associated with an event. The compiled template is a Bayesian-based mathematical model, devised exclusively by the user-client without the need for expert assistance.

The data, so assembled and accrued, can be monitored for statistical significance, which is displayed in graphic form. At a certain point, the template becomes a bonafide artificial intelligence tool, which gains in intelligence automatically as more data is submitted.

The full series of key features is now realized:

Incorporation of Bayesian probability into a functional web-enabled model.

Refinement of probabilities of various outcome alternatives - not simply their presence or absence - by addressing independent criteria that lead to a cumulative result in real time.

Facilitates customization of all contributory evidence-based data at the user interface. The database thereby created has global applicability yet local administration.

Entering data for creation of the template does not require an expert in the field, nor familiarity with information technology/knowledge engineering.

Serves to create and support industry standards via a fully transactional i strategy for any given business scenario such as utilization management.

Ability to create new subsets of questions and answers with their corresponding weighted averages or entirely new algorithms using the template.

Changing one particular answer within a typical 'if...then' software decision tool requires backtracking. The functionality of ePhysicians Office, Inc.'s tabular analysis allows for changes in a single answer anywhere in the algorithm followed by immediate recompilation of probabilities of all outcomes.

These concepts of unique design and technology have been forged into patented software at ePhysicians Office, Inc.

The result is substantial benefit to the client in service, outcome and cost.
